Hey! Hey! We're The Monkees...

The Monkees are an American/English rock and pop band, originally active between 1966 and 1971, but still going strong today. Originally formed in Los Angeles in 1965 by Bob Rafelson and Bert Schneider for the American television series "The Monkees", a musicla sit-com. The series aired from 1966 to 1968; but has been syndicated and repeated all over the world since. If you look, you will still find it airing somewhere today!

The quartet composes of Americans Micky Dolenz, Michael Nesmith, and Peter Tork; and Englishman Davy Jones. The band's music was initially supervised by producer Don Kirshner, backed by the songwriting duo of Tommy Boyce and Bobby Hart (though some of their biggest commercial hits were penned by the now legendary artist Neil Diamond.

The tv series saw a revival in the mid 1980's, which led to the first set of reunion concert tours, as well as new studio albums. The Monkees have reunited and toured several times since... and are still going strong today...

The initial draw of The Monkees was their 1960's tv serives; many people at the time liking them to an American Beatles. But along with, and after, the series came their own success; out-lasting many of the other bands that started in the 60's, including The Beatles! ... Combined, The Monkees first five studio albums reached a total of 15 times Platinum. Do date, The Monkees has sold over 75 mllion recoreds worldwide.
